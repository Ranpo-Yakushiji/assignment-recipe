Pepper Lunch

Ingredients:
  1. 250g white rice
  2. 150g chipped beef
  3. 2 big spoon corn
  4. 2 small spoon butter
  5. chopped green onion( prefesences)
  6. black pepper (prefer quantities)
    • Sauce
      1. 30cc sake
      2. 2 big spoon mirin
      3. 1/2 big spoon sugar
      4. 2 big spoon soy sauce
    • Choosable
      1. pizza cheese

How To Do?
  1. Pour sake, mirin, sugar, soy sauce and mashed garlic into a bowl and still it well. This is the backup sauce.
  2. Pour 4 big spoon pf sauce into another bowl and add the chipped beef then mix it up. Pickled it for 5 minutes.
  3. Put rice into a bowl and press it. Heat the pan and put butter on it, then undercut the rice and don't touch it.
  4. Put the beef that was pickled beside rice.
  5. Add corns, chopped green onion and sprinkle black pepper on it.
  6. You can add cheese on it if you like it.
