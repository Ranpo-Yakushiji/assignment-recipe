Crepes Berries Batter


Ingredients:
  • Batter
    1. 2 eggs
    2. 105g milk
    3. 70g water
    4. 140g plain four
    5. 1g salt
    6. 36g melted unsalted butter

  • Inside
    1. 3 strawberries
    2. 80g fresh whipped cream(divided into 3 times to use)
    3. 24g sugar

  • Surface
    1. 6 small blueberries
    2. 6 raspberries
    3. 1 strawberry
    4. fresh whipped cream(dispensable)
    5. white sugar powder(random)
How To Do?
  1. Mix the ingredients of batter together until well.

  2. Leave it for 20 minutes.

  3. Use middile-small fire to heat the pan and brush pan's surface with one layer cooking oil. Pour 24g batter evenly distrubuted on pan, fried 30 seconds, turn over and fried 10 seconds.

  4. Take it out and fried next one. Repeat step 3 until finished.

  5. Stir the fresh whipped cre4am with high speed egg blender for 20 seconds. Add 12g sugar and stir again for 30 seconds. Then, add 12g sugar and stir it again until frost.

  6. Take a slice of pie crust, put 3 sliced strawberries, squeeze cream on it, roll it up and put on a plate.
  7. Put all the blueberries, raspberries, strawberry, fresh whipped cream and white sugar powder according personal preference.

Golden Bread

Ingredients:
  1. 3 eggs
  2. 20g milk
  3. 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  4. 5 slices of bread
  5. a litte bit of pepper
How To Do?
  1. Break eggs and whisk eggs.
  2. Mix the pepper (according to own prefesences), milk and sugar into the egg liquid.
  3. Dip 2 faces od breads into the mixture.
  4. Fried 2 faces of breads until cooked.
